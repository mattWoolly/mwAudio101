## Objective

Implement the linear-control-current OTA taper and tanh soft-drive: out = tanh(kVcaOtaDrive*taper(control)*in)/tanh(kVcaOtaDrive) with taper(c)=pow(clamp(c,0,1),kVcaTaperExp).
